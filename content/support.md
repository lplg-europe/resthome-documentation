# Support and assistance

:::{rh-description}
Contact LPLG support for Resthome (nursing home software for MR/MRS) — open a ticket, what information to include, and where to find help.
:::

A problem or a question about Resthome? Here is how to get help, from the fastest
to the most direct.

## 1. Search the documentation first

Most answers are already here:

- The **search** at the top of the page (magnifying glass icon): type a keyword, a **code**
  (e.g. "931", "MDA"), or an error message.
- The [FAQ](faq.md) and the [Glossary](glossaire.md).
- The topic guides: [billing](facturation/index.md),
  [eFact](ehealth/efact.md), [insurability (MDA)](ehealth/mda.md),
  [agreements (eAgreement)](ehealth/eagreement.md).

The troubleshooting pages resolve most common cases:
[eFact rejections](ehealth/efact-rejets.md), [MDA errors](ehealth/mda-erreurs.md),
[Agreement refused](ehealth/eagreement-refus.md).

## 2. Open a ticket with LPLG support

If the documentation is not enough, contact our team:

- **Email: [support@lplg.eu](mailto:support@lplg.eu)** — each message automatically opens
  a tracking **ticket**.
- The **Support** button at the bottom right of every page brings you here.

:::{admonition} For a fast reply, please specify
:class: tip

- The **name of your facility**.
- What you were doing and the **screen** involved — a **screenshot** helps a lot.
- The exact **error message** or the **code** (eFact, MDA, eAgreement…).
- The billing **period** or the **resident** involved (internal reference).
:::

:::{admonition} Sensitive data (GDPR)
:class: warning

Avoid sending a **NISS** or complete medical data by email unless necessary. An
**anonymized screenshot** is usually enough — hide the name and the national
number.
:::

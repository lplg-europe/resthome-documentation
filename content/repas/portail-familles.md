# Family portal and kiosk

:::{rh-description}
The family portal and kiosk display of meals in Resthome — menus viewable online, QR code, full-screen display in the dining room.
:::

Resthome lets you **share menus** beyond the team: with **families** and directly **in the dining room**.

## Family portal

Relatives can view **the day's meals** and the history through a dedicated **portal** access. It's a trusted link: the family sees what the resident eats, without access to the rest of the record.

## Public pages and QR code

- The **weekly menu** can be published on a viewable page.
- A **printable version** with a **QR code** makes it possible to display the menu and point back to its online version, always up to date.

## Kiosk mode

**Kiosk mode** displays the menu in **full screen**, ideal for a screen or a television in the **dining room**. Residents and staff see the current meal at a glance, with no interaction needed.

:::{admonition} Always up to date
:class: tip

Since everything comes from the same menus in Resthome, the portal, the public page and the kiosk **automatically** display the latest version — no double entry.
:::

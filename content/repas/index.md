The **Meals** application organises the establishment's catering: **menus**,
**dishes**, **diets** tailored to each resident, **nutritional follow-up** and
**hydration**, through to the **family portal** and the dining-room display.

## The dashboard

The **Meals dashboard** gives the day's overview: planned services, dietary
alerts, hydration to watch.

## What the application covers

- **Menus, diets and hydration** — Compose the menus, manage diets and
  allergies, follow nutrition and hydration.
  [→ Menus and diets](menus-regimes.md)
- **Family portal and kiosk** — Families view the meals; the dining room
  displays the menu full-screen.
  [→ Portal and kiosk](portail-familles.md)

## Main menus

- **Operations** — Menus, meal services, distribution, **dietary alerts**,
  hydration.
- **Catalogue** — dishes, dish categories, products.
- **Statistics** — catering reporting.
- **Configuration** — diets, allergies, meal types.

:::{admonition} A dietitian role
:class: note

A **dietitian** profile lets you entrust the management of menus, diets and
nutritional follow-up to the people concerned, without opening up all the rest
of Resthome to them.
:::
